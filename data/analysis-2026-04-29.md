## 6. Post-Deploy Fix Verification

### Fix 1: Per-strategy consecutive-loss cooldown
- **Before:** Global cooldown blocked ALL strategies after any 3 consecutive losses.
- **After:** Each strategy has its own `_consecutive_losses_by_strategy` counter.
- **Evidence:** `2026-04-29 11:15 SOLUSDT supertrend_rsi APPROVED` — first approval after the new container was deployed, confirming supertrend_rsi is not blocked by other strategies' losses.

### Fix 2: Minimum stop distance guard (min_stop_distance_pct = 0.5%)
- **Before:** Ultra-tight ATR-derived stops (< 0.5% from entry) were accepted and caused instant SL_HIT losses.
- **After:** Any stop tighter than 0.5% is rejected with `TIGHT STOP` reason.
- **Evidence:**
  - `2026-04-29 10:15 ETHUSDT LONG — TIGHT STOP — 0.42% < 0.5%` ← rejected correctly
  - `2026-04-29 10:45 ETHUSDT LONG — TIGHT STOP — 0.48% < 0.5%` ← rejected correctly

### Known Issue: ReduceOnly Order Rejection at 12:17
- `[Executor] Market order failed: SOLUSDT SELL — ReduceOnly Order is rejected.`
- This is a race condition: Binance exchange-side SL was already triggered and position closed before the bot's reduce-only close order arrived.
- The bot correctly detected the position as closed (via `pnl_tracker`) and updated state to `SL_HIT, PnL=-1.97`.
- This is a benign race condition, not a new bug. No action needed.

## 7. Issues to Investigate / Tune

### Issue 1: ema_adx_volume consistently losing (-$5.43 avg per trade)
- 9 trades in the recent 10 days, all SL_HIT losses in the -$1.74 to -$2.30 range.
- Signal confidence is high (79–91) but trades are losing immediately after entry.
- The strategy is being triggered during trending regimes but stops are getting hit before TP.
- **Possible root cause:** ATR-based stop may be too tight for the measured volatility. Strategy may need a wider stop multiplier or higher minimum R:R.
- **Suggested action:** Either raise `min_net_risk_reward` specifically for `ema_adx_volume` or add a strategy-level `atr_multiplier` config parameter.

### Issue 2: Breakeven stop wiping out fees
- 8 of the last 21 supertrend_rsi trades show exactly -$0.27 (fee-only loss) from BREAKEVEN_STOP.
- This suggests `breakeven_activation_r=0.8` fires too early — price barely touches the 0.8R level and then reverses.
- **Suggested action:** Raise `breakeven_activation_r` from 0.8 to 1.0–1.2, or add a small buffer (`breakeven_buffer_pct: 0.1`) so the SL moves to entry + small buffer instead of exactly entry.

### Issue 3: liquidity_sweep_orderflow — 0 profitable trades
- 2 trades, both SL_HIT, -$3.04 total. One at 0.5m duration (instant SL) and one at 62m.
- Strategy was only recently added; too few samples to conclude.
- **Watch:** Need more data. The 98.9-confidence approval at 01:15 ended in SL_HIT — could be false signal during ranging market.

### Issue 4: TIME_STOP consistently losses
- 9 TIME_STOP trades totaling -$19.37. The 4-hour stop kicks in for directionless trades.
- These trades tied up capital and exited at a loss. May be better to reduce the `time_stop_hours` from 4 to 2 hours, or add a "progress check" — exit earlier if still at < 10% of TP.
